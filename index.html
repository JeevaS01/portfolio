Hi! I'm Jeeva S — a freshly minted Data Scientist and AI/ML Engineer from Chidambaram, Tamil Nadu. I hold a Master of Data Science from IITM Pravartak (GUVI) and a M.Sc. Computer Science from Annamalai University, combining rigorous academic training with real-world project experience.
